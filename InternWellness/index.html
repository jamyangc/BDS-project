Meet Jamyang: the intern behind this project

1. Introduction

My name is Jamyang Chozom, and I am doing my internship at Bhutan Data Scientist Pvt. Ltd. At the beginning of my internship, my project focused on the integration of Artificial Intelligence in credit default analysis. I always wanted to become an analyst, so I was excited to work on a technical topic and apply my academic knowledge in a real-world setting.

2. Initial Internship Experience

As my internship progressed, I began to notice something beyond technical work. Many interns, including myself, were experiencing stress and burnout, yet very few openly talked about it. I was also going through similar challenges. Managing responsibilities alongside personal commitments became increasingly difficult, leading to missed deadlines and additional pressure.

3. Realization

This became a turning point for me. I realized that I did not just want to overcome stress, but also use it as an opportunity for growth. I also wanted to support others going through similar experiences. Stress began to feel less like an individual problem and more like a shared issue.

4. Change of Project Focus

I decided to shift my project from AI-based credit default analysis to intern stress and wellbeing. This felt more meaningful and relevant to real experiences. It led to the creation of a platform designed to support interns in managing stress and improving wellbeing.

5. Solution Development

The platform was designed to transform personal insights into practical support. It provides simple tools and resources such as relaxation techniques, breathing exercises, and guided content to help interns manage stress in their daily lives.

6. Scope and Impact

Although inspired by interns in Bhutan, the challenges addressed are universal. Stress, burnout, and emotional wellbeing are common among young professionals globally, making this solution applicable beyond geographical boundaries.

7. Learning Outcome

This journey helps me understand people, their struggles, and how data and technology can be used to create meaningful impact. I gained practical skills and a deeper appreciation of human-centered problem solving.

about project

This project was created to highlight the often unseen challenges interns face during their professional journey. While internships are meant to be a learning experience, many interns struggle with stress, pressure, and emotional wellbeing.

This platform shares stories, insights, and simple tools to help interns better understand and manage their stress. It combines personal experiences with practical support such as breathing exercises, music, and meditation.

Created as part of my internship journey, this project reflects both lived experience and a desire to support others in similar situations.
