Before you enter into main page meet Jamyang: the intern behind this project

1. Introduction

My name is Jamyang Chozom, and I have done my six month professional internship at Bhutan Data Scientist Pvt. Ltd At the beginning of my internship, my project focused on the integration of Artificial Intelligence in credit default analysis. I always wanted to become an analyst, so I was excited to work on a technical topic and learn new things and explore beyond the classroom means.

2. Initial Internship Experience

As my internship progressed, I began to notice something beyond technical work. Many interns, including myself, were experiencing stress, yet very few openly talked about it. I was also going through similar challenges. Managing responsibilities alongside personal commitments became increasingly difficult, leading to missed deadlines and additional pressure.

3. Realization

This became a turning point for me. I realized that I did not just want to overcome stress, but also use it as an opportunity for growth. I also wanted to support others going through similar experiences. Stress began to feel less like an individual problem and more like a shared issue.

4. Change of Project Focus

I decided to shift my project from credit default analysis to intern stress and wellbeing. This felt more meaningful, relevant for myself and representing my friends. That moment of feeling distress, feelings of giving up and challenges led to the creation of a platform designed to support interns in managing stress and improving wellbeing.

5. Solution Development

The platform was designed to transform lived experience into practical support. This website will try to provide simple tools and resources such as relaxation techniques, breathing exercises, and guided content to help interns not only manage stress in their daily lives but also use stress for their personal growth.

6. Scope and Impact

Although data collected are from lived experiences of Gedu College of Business Studies' interns, the challenges addressed are universal. Stress, burnout, and wellbeing are common among professional settings, making this solution applicable beyond geographical boundaries.

7. Learning Outcome

This journey helps me understand people, their struggles, and how data and technology can be used to create meaningful impact. I gained practical skills and a deeper appreciation of human-centered problem solving.

Always remember the following

🗣️

Talk to your friends, elders and parents

Whenever stress starts to build up, don't keep it to yourself. Talk to your friends, your family, or anyone you trust. The people who care about you want to be there for you.

📿

Pause & Pray

Pause, breathe, and turn to prayer. In silence, you may find clarity and strength. Let peace slowly find its way to you.

🌱

You Are Still Learning

You are in a learning process and that is completely okay. Growth takes time and every experience, good or bad, is teaching you something valuable.

⏳

Take a Break

Pause for a moment and give yourself rest. Step away, stretch, or close your eyes. Sip something warm and breathe slowly. Small breaks can make a big difference.

about project

This project was created to highlight the often unseen challenges interns face during their professional journey. While internships are meant to be a learning experience, many interns struggle with stress, pressure, and emotional wellbeing.

This platform shares stories, insights, and simple tools to help interns better understand and manage their stress. It combines personal experiences with practical support such as breathing exercises, music, and meditation.

Created as part of my internship journey, this project reflects both lived experience and a desire to support others in similar situations.
