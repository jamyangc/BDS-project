About my intern project

I am currently undertaking an internship in Bhutan Data Scientist, where I am exploring the stress and wellbeing of interns of gcbs. My primary project focuses on analyzing the stress levels and overall well-being of interns at the Gedu College of Business Studies (GCBS). Through this project, I aim to identify the key factors contributing to intern stress, understand how these factors impact performance and learning, and provide actionable insights to improve internship programs. This experience allows me to apply my skills in data analysis, research, and visualization while gaining valuable exposure to organizational dynamics and human behavior in professional settings.
