GCBS Intern Wellbeing

This website focuses on stress and well-being experienced by interns during their internship period. It is based on real data collected from GCBS interns as part of an internship project. The aim is to raise awareness and share insights that can be useful for interns, educators, and anyone interested in intern well-being.

The intended audience for this website includes interns, students, educators, and the general public who are interested in understanding stress and well-being during internships.
